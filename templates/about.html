Purpose: Generate geophylogenies

Description: Converts a phylogenetic tree and the tip taxa's geographic coordinates into a KML file for Google Earth viewing. This is the work of Andrew W. Hill, Brian Stucky, and Robert Guralnick. We will be working to add many new features as well as integrate the project with another of ours, PhyloBox.
